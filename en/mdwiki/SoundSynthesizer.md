## Hardware Description

 * ATMega328
 * 12 buttons, with 8 arranged in a row for a one-octave keyboard
 * 8 LEDs
 * Serial DAC (12 bits)
 * Audio amp w/volume control
 * Small speaker
 * light sensor for download from web
 * ISP connector
 * USBAsp connector
